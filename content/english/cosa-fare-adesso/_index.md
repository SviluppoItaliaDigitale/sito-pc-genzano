## What is happening?

Identify the situation you are experiencing. Click on the matching link to see the appropriate operational guidance (Italian pages — use your browser translator if needed):

- 🌍 [**Earthquake**](/rischi-prevenzione/rischio-sismico/) — seismic shake felt
- 🌧️ [**Flood or flooding**](/rischi-prevenzione/rischio-idrogeologico/) — high water, landslide, mudslide
- 🔥 [**Fire**](/rischi-prevenzione/rischio-incendio/) — wildfire or visible urban fire
- 💨 [**Strong wind**](/rischi-prevenzione/vento-forte/) — violent gusts, falling branches
- ⛈️ [**Severe storm**](/rischi-prevenzione/temporali-intensi/) — lightning, hail, intense rainfall
- ⚡ [**Blackout**](/rischi-prevenzione/blackout/) — no electricity

## General rules always valid

1. **Don't panic.** Think calmly and act safely.
2. **Call 112** to report dangerous situations.
3. **Do not hinder rescuers.** Do not go to the emergency site out of curiosity.
4. **Follow only official sources.** Do not spread unverified information.
5. **Help those in need** if you can do so safely (elderly, children, people with disabilities).
6. **Keep your phone charged** and use it only for necessary communications.
